Who we are

Since 1997 we have been a passionate advocate for the preservartion and conservation of Aotearoa's unique native species and ecosystems.

Our dedicated team of scientists, conservationists, volunteers, and supporters collaborate across disciplines and communities to implement effective strategies for species recovery and habitat preservation. Through innovative projects, partnerships, and public outreach, we strive to empower individuals and organizations to become stewards of our precious natural heritage.

Together, we can make a difference. Join us in our commitment to safeguarding New Zealand's native species for generations to come. Together, let's ensure that our land remains a sanctuary for biodiversity and a source of inspiration for all.

Thank you for supporting Aotearoa Conservation Alliance in our vital mission to protect and preserve the unique wonders of Aotearoa. Together, we can create a future where nature thrives and Aotearoa's native species continue to enchant and inspire us all.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple yet profound: to protect and restore the delicate balance of our ecosystems by preserving the habitats and populations of native flora and fauna.

Our Goal

A sustainble & brighter future for us all, ensuring that our land remains a sanctuary for biodiversity and cultivate a bigger better tomorrow, an Aotearoa where both humans and wildlife thrive.

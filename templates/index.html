Who are we?

Founded in 1997 we are the Aotearoa Conservation Alliance, we've successfully boosted population of many endangered Aotearoa Native Species

We have successfully returned many native species to the wild and increased the population of Kiwi by 100%, Kea by 150% and Kākāpō by 50%

We are an inclusive and diverse team who all look out for one another and are proud of the work we do to protect our beautiful Aotearoa

We ensure that animals return safely to the wild and have extensive procedures in place to protect our species from endangerement or extinction

And we do all of this while still caring for our environment and ensuring we don't do stuff that can put other species at risk
